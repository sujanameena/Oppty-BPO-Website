Healthcare and Life Sciences Analytics Services

Competitive Intelligence (CI)

Research and competitive insights are critical requirements for strategic decision-making in healthcare and pharmaceutical companies.

OPPTY’ sophisticated SaaS-based CI solution provides 360° support by streamlining knowledge collation through public and commercial data sources, intelligent curation and dissemination to end-users through preferred channels.

Business Intelligence (BI) and Visualization

Healthcare and life sciences companies need new analytical approaches to tap into data and unlock insights for brand strategies, competitor analysis, new launches, patients’ needs and inventory planning.

OPPTY’s BI and Visualization solutions enhance data engineering and visualization capabilities of organizations to establish best-in-class customer-focused functions.

Marketing Analytics

Decision-making in the industry is dependent on huge volumes of highly disparate data from multiple sources. Whether for marketing campaigns or patient enablement, companies need to look not only to the past but also to future — and plan what is likely to happen.

OPPTY’s Marketing Analytics leverages diverse data sources to extract valuable insights for actionable decision-making. With expertise in healthcare and pharma industries and advanced analytical algorithms, we assist organizations with measuring promotional effectiveness, marketing-mix analytics and in-depth understanding of patient dynamics.

Sales Analytics

Success in sales requires customized strategies and real-time insights to maximize customer engagement and drive performance. Life sciences companies need a highly informed and motivated sales force to deliver commercial excellence.

OPPTY’s Sales Analytics helps clients with go-to-market strategies, commercial team sizing and structures, customer engagement and targeting strategies, territory alignment and call planning, incentive compensation programs, performance management and field reporting.

R&D Analytics & Insights

Research insights play a vital role for pharmaceutical companies in product planning, due-diligence activities in mergers and acquisitions, and more.

OPPTY Analytics’s comprehensive research insights assimilate information from various data sources to drive a holistic picture and deliver teed-up insights.
